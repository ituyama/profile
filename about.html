About This Dojo

CoderDojo Sanomaru is a free programming class for local children.
The goal is to create an environment where children from wealthy families
and those who don't have the same access to a programming environment.
Dojo's mentors are all student-run, and we believe in learning from each other!
Our goal is to create a world where every child is able to follow his or
her dreams without being bound by his or her surroundings.
